Our Story

The Lady &The Voice

"We believe that relational growth isn't just for romantic couples — it's for anyone who wants to be seen, heard, and understood."

Eileen and Armand — known as The Lady and The Voice — are Relational Awareness Coaches and an Empower Couple committed to helping individuals, couples, and communities build deeper, healthier relationships.

Work With Us

Decades of Experience, One Shared Mission

With combined experience in education, media, public speaking, entertainment, nonprofit leadership, and coaching, Eileen and Armand have built a movement rooted in empathy, communication, and personal accountability.

Through their signature L.U.V Logically Speaking™ program, they teach people how to better understand themselves and others — one conversation and one connection at a time.

They've been featured alongside Grammy winners, celebrated athletes, public officials, and community leaders — yet their hearts remain firmly planted in the communities they serve across the Bay Area and beyond.

See Our Services

The Lady — Eileen

Eileen, "The Lady," is an accomplished entrepreneur, author and highly sought-after wedding officiant who crafts heartfelt ceremonies that honor love, authenticity, and connection. With her warm, genuine approach, Eileen inspires others to embrace self-discovery, celebrate their relationships, and approach life with courage and purpose.

The Voice — Armand

Armand, known as "The Voice," has spent nearly 30 years as a radio personality specializing in relationship radio, most notably as the host of The Quiet Storm. Armand uses the power of music and storytelling to set the tone for intimacy and reflection, creating a space where listeners can slow down, connect with their emotions, and rediscover the beauty of romance. For countless individuals and couples, The Quiet Storm has been a soundtrack for love, healing, and meaningful conversations, reminding listeners of the role that intention and vulnerability play in building stronger relationships.

Together — An Empower Couple

Together, Armand and Eileen combine their personal and professional experiences to help others ask the hard questions: How are we showing up in our relationships? Are we contributing to the connection- or the conflict? Their unique approach centers on Listen, Understand, and Validate (L.U.V), equipping individuals, couples, and families to cultivate stronger, healthier connections.

Beyond their work as coaches, Armand and Eileen lead their nonprofit organization, ATU Academy, (www.atuacademy.org) which equips young men and women of color with the tools, mentorship, and confidence to succeed academically, personally, and professionally.

With a shared commitment to open communication, mutual respect, and accountability, The Lady and The Voice invite you on a journey of reflection, growth, and transformation- one that will not only strengthen your relationships but also change how you engage with the world around you.
